ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Shiena, a Licensed Geologist and Data Analyst from the Philippines.

After earning my Bachelor’s Degree in Geology from the University of Southeastern Philippines in 2018, I started my professional journey as a geologist in a research firm. Currently, I work as an environmental geologist in a government agency.

I became interested in Data Analytics in 2022 and began taking online courses and watching tutorial videos to explore this field. The ability to use data to create narratives and influence decisions is what excites me, and that’s why I chose to follow a career in Data Analysis.

I love indulging in arts and crafts, and traveling is another passion of mine. During the pandemic, I started a resin crafts business and created vlogs on my YouTube channel, Resin Crafts 101, which attracted hundred thousand of views. I've also had the opportunity to explore many local and international destinations, and travelling is one of the most fulfilling experiences for me. It allows me to explore new cultures, people, and cuisines, giving me a broader view of the world.
